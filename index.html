What is Bakfy

Bakfy is a mobile app developed by bunch of alums from premier engineering colleges in India. It acts as local broadcast for colleges where you can chit-chat about the happenings in your college or on any other topic you wish to. Bakfy puts the entire college under one roof, without any friend requests/following someone/etc. And if you feel what you want to post might be too spicy, then there is the option of posting anonymously as well ;)
